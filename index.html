About

Hey, I’m a Seattle-based tattoo artist specializing in fineline work and Asian-inspired designs. My focus is on creating clean, detailed pieces that reflect your vision while maintaining a timeless aesthetic. I work out of a private studio in West Seattle—an inviting, laid-back space where you can feel comfortable and heard. Whether you’re after something subtle or a statement piece, I’m here to collaborate and bring your ideas to life. Ready to get started?
